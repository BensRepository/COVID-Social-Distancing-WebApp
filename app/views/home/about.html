What does our site provide? why is it so amazing!

Our website offers a means to stay inside during the global pandemic. You can play games and try beat the global highscores or join our chat room and communicate with others struggling with lockdown. Lastly, you'll be able to read and interact in our categoried forum to see what you can do during lockdown

Our Goals and the future

We strive to give members a enjoyable experience while locked in doors. We aim to give the most recent covid updates to keep members at ease. As a member we want to bring more fun into lives! and in doing so hope you will stick around for the future where we will grow our community to help tackle future ordeals

And lastly, Feel free to contact us We dont bite!

As new developers we understand not everything we do it correct - feel free to contact us to get in touch if you have any suggestions or concerns with how we operate.
